How Shares & Luck Work

What is a share? Every time your miner finds a hash below the pool difficulty target, it submits a share as proof of work. Shares do not win Bitcoin directly — they prove your miner is genuinely hashing.

Why are share counts large? CKPool counts difficulty-weighted shares. A share at difficulty 1,000 counts as 1,000. At 10,000 it counts as 10,000. This gives a more accurate picture of total work than raw submission counts.

What wins a block? A block is found when a share hash falls below the network difficulty target. Your best share shows how close you have gotten. Every hash has an equal independent chance regardless of history.
